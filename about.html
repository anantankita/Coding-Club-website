About Us

Powered by us The Students at SRMAP

Coding club, SRM- University AP is a student-run community exclusive to the students of SRM- University, AP.

The aim of this club is to learn the art of coding and increase the joy of programming. The goal of this club is to cherish the appetite for learning modern technologies and shape them through programming.

YOU CAN do this !!

We are a bunch of welcoming, friendly coding enthusiasts. Here we try to take away the fear of programming from the members of the club.

We try to inspire and motivate members to excel in life with the help of coding. We also aim to provide a platform to nourish the technical skills of its members through various activities.

During these activities, you can learn about new technologies, showcase their skills and explain concepts to the rest of the members so that everyone can learn and grow together.

WE HAVE YOUR BACK

We facilitate members with the knowledge, confidence, and opportunity to make a difference in the world. The club's major goal is to strengthen the programming skills of its members.
